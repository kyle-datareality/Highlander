What We Do

Our Services

Residential Roofing

Protect your home with expert residential roofing. From single-shingle repairs to complete re-roofs, we use premium IKO and Owens Corning products rated for Alberta's weather.

Commercial Roofing

Calgary businesses rely on our expertise to keep properties secure. We deliver durable commercial solutions using IKO and Owens Corning materials built for long-term performance.

Siding

Boost curb appeal and energy efficiency with premium siding. Whether you need a full upgrade or targeted repairs, we deliver results that stand up to Calgary winters.

Fascia and Soffit

Complete your exterior with professional fascia and soffit installation. Protect your roofline, improve ventilation, and give your property a polished, finished appearance.

About Us

Family Owned.
Calgary Built.

Highlander Roofing is a family-owned and operated roofing company based in Calgary, Alberta. We built this business on a simple idea: keep overhead low, hire skilled people, and pass the savings on to our customers without cutting corners.

We are licensed, insured, and WCB certified, serving homeowners and businesses throughout Calgary and the surrounding area. Every project comes with transparent pricing, clear communication, and our personal commitment to quality work done on time.

Founded in Calgary
Highlander Roofing established as a family-owned contractor in Southwest Calgary.
